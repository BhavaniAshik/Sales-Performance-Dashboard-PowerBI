# 📌 Project Overview

The **Sales Performance Dashboard** is an interactive Power BI report designed to monitor sales performance, profitability, customer rankings, and category-wise performance. It provides business users with real-time KPIs, year-over-year comparisons, sales trends, and customer insights to support strategic decision-making.

**Reporting Period:** January 2024 – September 2026

---

# 🎯 Business Objective

The objective of this dashboard is to:

- Monitor overall sales performance.
- Track Sales YTD and Profit YTD.
- Compare Year-over-Year (YoY) growth.
- Analyze monthly sales trends.
- Evaluate category-wise sales and profitability.
- Identify top-performing customers.
- Support business decisions through interactive analytics.

# 🔍 Business Insights

## 1. Strong Revenue Performance

The business generated **25.57 Million** in total sales, demonstrating consistent revenue growth throughout the reporting period.

---

## 2. Impressive Year-over-Year Growth

Sales increased by **57.73%** compared to the previous year, indicating strong business expansion and effective sales strategies.

**Recommendation**

Continue investing in successful sales channels while monitoring sustainable growth.

---

## 3. Healthy Profitability

Total Profit reached **15.29 Million**, with **4.33 Million** generated during the current year (Profit YTD), reflecting efficient cost management.

---

## 4. Electronics Drives Revenue

Electronics is the highest-performing category, contributing the largest share of total sales.

**Recommendation**

Maintain sufficient inventory and introduce targeted promotions for Electronics products.

---

## 5. Furniture Leads Profit Contribution

Although Electronics generates the highest revenue, Furniture contributes the largest percentage of category profit, indicating stronger margins.

---

## 6. Seasonal Sales Patterns

Monthly sales fluctuate across the reporting period, with noticeable peak months.

**Recommendation**

Use seasonal trends for demand forecasting, inventory planning, and marketing campaigns.

---

## 7. Customer Concentration

The dashboard identifies top-performing customers, enabling the business to recognize high-value customers and develop personalized retention strategies.

---

## 8. Average Order Value

The Average Order Value (AOV) is **25.57K**, indicating strong revenue per transaction.

**Recommendation**

Increase AOV further through product bundles, cross-selling, and upselling.

---

## 9. Category Performance Comparison

Comparing category sales and profit helps identify products that generate high revenue versus those delivering higher profitability.

---

## 10. Interactive Business Monitoring

The dashboard enables users to analyze KPIs dynamically using category and date filters, allowing faster and more informed business decisions.
